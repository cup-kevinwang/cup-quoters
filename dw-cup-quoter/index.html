Pricing Notes
  • Packing: Base on yellow carton. White carton: +US$0.10/ctn
  • Container: Base on 40HQ. If 20GP: 4oz +$0.20 / 6-7oz +$0.30 / 8oz +$0.40 / 10-22oz +$0.50 /ctn
  • Printing: Base on 1-3 colors. Extra color: 4-8oz +$0.10 / 10-22oz +$0.20 /ctn
  • Fan (Innercup) GSM: ±10 gsm per step. Inner cup paper. Affects paper cost by ratio.
  • Outwall GSM: ±10 gsm per step. Outer wall paper. Affects paper cost by ratio.
  • Fan/Wall/Bottom price: RMB per ton. Affects paper cost directly.
Formula
  • Paper cost = cp0 × newMetric / origMetric (3-component ratio)
  • newMetric = FanPrice × InnerGSM × InnerArea + WallPrice × OutwallGSM × OutwallArea + BottomPrice × BottomGSM × BottomArea
  • Cost/Print ctn = Paper + Print-Cut + Form-Pack-Trans + Depre + PumpE/Qty40HC
  • Cost/White ctn = Paper + Form-Pack-Trans + Depre + PumpE/Qty40HC
  • Price = Cost / ExRate + Margin / (Qty40HC × ExRate)
